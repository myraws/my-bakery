Heart-full breads

homemade and freshly baked

Amongst the quiet neighbourhood of Northern Tokyo finds our secret spot of bread paradise at the top floor of a humbly little villa. my-bakery runs twice a week and serves freshly baked bread at its finest quality. It experiments with new flavours every now and then to discover interesting combinations of natural ingredients. Through baking, it wishes to bring joy and healing to those who are weary and burdened.

"I’d like to be a part of something with a bit of soul. I really want to know about the people I’m serving, what makes them smile."

We open at a irregular basis so feel free to pop in your requests and come visit my-bakery at your convenience.

my-bakery serves Japanese-style breads and bakes with local ingredients. One of the signatures is あんぱん (red bean bun) which uses Hokkaido Tokachi chunky red bean paste as the filling.

Seasonal breads are served occasionally based on the availability of ingredients. In Spring this year, my-bakery crafted the Sakura bread with cherry blossom flavoured filling. Its fragrant scent mixes with a light trace of salty flavour which gives it a unique taste.

my-bakery aims to serve good-quality breads that is fresh and healthy. Breads are made from hand-picked local ingredients and are kept to its original taste as much as possible by reducing the amount of sugar and butter used in the filling. It hopes to retain the natural taste of ingredients used and satisfy the sweet tooth without bringing a sense of guilt.

All breads are crafted with a heart-full of love. Leave the worries behind and treat yourself with a humble and gentle taste of the angel's whisper - a deliciously and freshly baked bread as said in French.

We are 15 minutes walk from the closest metro station and 5 minutes walk from several bus stops. Feel free to drop by and say hello :)
